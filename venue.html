Facilities

The following facilities are available at all NeuroFriends meetings.

Drinks: Tea*, Coffee* & Sugar, Milk & Water

Toilets: Toilets are available

Access: Our venue is accessible to all, it has a ramp to gain access to the main building

If you have any specific access requirements, please contact a facilitator.
