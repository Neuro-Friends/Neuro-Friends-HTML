Policies

We want every session to run as smoothly as possible, so we’ve put together a few simple guidelines for all attendees to follow during their stay.

Safeguarding Adults

The Adult Safeguarding Policy for NeuroFriends outlines our commitment to protecting adults with care and support needs from abuse, neglect, and exploitation. It provides clear guidance for volunteers on how to identify, respond to, and report safeguarding concerns. This policy ensures we promote safety, uphold dignity, and act swiftly and responsibly to support those at risk—because everyone deserves to feel safe and respected.

Bullying & Harassment

This policy is here to help keep NeuroFriends a safe, respectful, and supportive space for everyone involved—whether we're meeting face-to-face or chatting online. It sets out what behavior is expected, what’s not okay, and what steps we’ll take if someone feels uncomfortable or unsafe. Think of it as a shared agreement to look out for each other

Social Media Policy

This Social Media Policy is here to help keep our NeuroFriends community safe, respectful, and inclusive—both in person and online. It sets out simple, clear guidelines for how members and volunteers can use social media responsibly, including how we handle photos, videos, and personal content. Our goal is to protect everyone’s privacy and comfort while still celebrating the joy, creativity, and connection that makes NeuroFriends so special.
