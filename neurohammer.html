NeuroHammer

What is NeuroHammer?

We’re excited to share that we’ve received a generous donation of Warhammer models, some partially painted and others still in their boxes. These models will be available at future sessions for attendees to paint, customize, or even use to create games. While we may not have enough for a full army right now, we’re more than happy to help anyone interested in building one.

Currently, we don’t have any members who play Warhammer, but if this is something you’re interested in—or if you know someone who is Neurodivergent and might enjoy it—please feel free to pass this along!

This is an exciting step for NeuroFriends, and we hope this expansion will appeal to a wide range of people.

If you have any questions, feel free to email facilitators@neurofriends.net, and one of our team members will get back to you.

What We Offer

On top of all the things that NeuroFriends offers we also offer the following for people taking part in NeuroHammer

Models

A selection of models to paint, adapts and create an army with.

Paints

A selection of citadel paints to use in order to paint the models.

Tools

We have a selection of tools to help you make an army fit for battle.

3D Printing

We have the ability to print 3D models for the group should that be something that is required.

Frequently Asked Questions

People under the age of 18 can only attend NeuroFriends when accompanied by an adult or carer who has parental responsibility of them, they cannot be left unattended.

You don't need a diagnosis to attend NeuroFriends, we welcome everyone with or without a diagnosis.

NeuroFriends is a space for everyone, you are welcome to attend.

There will not be any recordings made during the meet ups, this is to protect the privacy of the members.

If you are unable to attend in person, please reach out to the facilitators using the contact form on OurSpace and we will make every effort to meet you individually or arrange a future meeting around your availability.
