What Is NeuroFriends?

NeuroFriends has been setup to support neurodivergent people. It is a peer to peer safe space for all.

In the NeuroFriends group everyone is equal and what is discussed within the group remains in the group.

You might be neurodivergent yourself, have a friend who is neurodivergent or be a parent of a neurodivergent person, you are welcome at NeuroFriends.

NeuroFriends welcomes EVERYONE

Frequently Asked Questions

People under the age of 18 can only attend NeuroFriends when accompanied by an adult or carer who has parental responsibility of them, they cannot be left unattended.

You don't need a diagnosis to attend NeuroFriends, we welcome everyone with or without a diagnosis.

NeuroFriends is a space for everyone, you are welcome to attend.

There will not be any recordings made during the meet ups, this is to protect the privacy of the members.

If you are unable to attend in person, please reach out to the facilitators using the contact form on OurSpace and we will make every effort to meet you individually or arrange a future meeting around your availability.
